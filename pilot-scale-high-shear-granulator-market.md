<p><p>The pilot scale high shear granulator market is segmented into horizontal and vertical types, each serving distinct processing needs. In contrast, vertical granulators have a vertical design that promotes gravity-assisted flow, often resulting in quicker processing times. Both types are essential for optimizing formulation processes in pharmaceuticals, food, and chemicals, offering flexibility for scaling up production while ensuring consistent product quality.</p></p>
