<p><strong>The Geiger-Mueller Counters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>End Window Type</li><li>Windowless Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Geiger-Mueller counters are specialized devices used for detecting ionizing radiation. The market is primarily segmented into two types: end window and windowless counters. End window counters feature a thin mica window, allowing for direct detection of alpha particles, making them suitable for alpha radiation measurement. In contrast, windowless counters have no such opening, primarily detecting beta and gamma radiation. Each type caters to specific applications based on the type of radiation being measured, influencing their usage in various fields like nuclear industry, healthcare, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
