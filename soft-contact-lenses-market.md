<p><strong>The Soft Contact Lenses Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Silicone Hydrogel</li><li>Hydrogels</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The soft contact lenses market is primarily divided into two types: silicone hydrogel and traditional hydrogels. Silicone hydrogel lenses are made from a breathable material that allows more oxygen to reach the cornea, enhancing comfort and eye health. They are ideal for extended wear. Traditional hydrogels, while offering good moisture retention, provide less oxygen permeability and are often preferred for daily wear. Both types cater to varying needs, including those for vision correction and comfort during prolonged usage.</p></p>
