<p><ul><li>Optical Micrograph</li><li>Electron Micrograph</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The micrographic camera market encompasses various types of imaging techniques, primarily optical micrographs and electron micrographs. Optical micrographs utilize visible light to capture detailed images of microscopic samples, ideal for biological and material studies. In contrast, electron micrographs employ electron beams, providing higher resolution images suitable for examining fine structures at the nanoscale. Both types serve critical roles in scientific research, quality control, and material analysis, catering to diverse applications across biology, materials science, and nanotechnology.</p></p>
