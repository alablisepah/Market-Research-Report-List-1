<p><p>The Metal & Glass Coatings market is segmented into solventborne, waterborne, and UV-cured coatings. Solventborne coatings contain organic solvents, offering excellent adhesion and durability but raise environmental concerns due to volatile organic compounds. Waterborne coatings use water as a solvent, providing a more eco-friendly option with lower emissions, although they may have slower drying times. UV-cured coatings utilize ultraviolet light to rapidly harden, providing a durable finish and quick application, making them suitable for high-speed production processes.</p></p>
