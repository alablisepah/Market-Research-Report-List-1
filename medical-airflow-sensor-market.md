<p><p>The medical airflow sensor market encompasses various types essential for monitoring and controlling air flow in healthcare settings. Vane airflow sensors utilize a rotating blade to measure airflow rates, while Karman vortex sensors detect changes in fluid dynamics caused by obstacles, providing precise measurements. Hot wire sensors measure temperature changes along a thin wire in airflow, offering high sensitivity. Hot film sensors operate similarly but use a thin film, enhancing durability and responsiveness, making them vital for accurate air handling in medical applications.</p></p>
