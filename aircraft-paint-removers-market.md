<p><p>The aircraft paint removers market is primarily divided into two types: water-based and solvent-based formulations. Water-based paint removers are environmentally friendly, offering low toxicity and ease of cleanup, making them suitable for various applications without harming the aircraft's surface. In contrast, solvent-based removers provide powerful degreasing and paint stripping capabilities, effectively handling tougher coatings but often involving higher volatility and potential health risks. Each type has distinct advantages, catering to different preferences within the aviation industry.</p></p>
