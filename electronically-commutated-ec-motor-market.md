<p><p>The Electronically Commutated (EC) Motor market is primarily divided into two types: inner rotor motors and outer rotor motors. Inner rotor motors have the rotor positioned inside the stator, providing better dynamic response and compact design, suitable for applications like HVAC systems and small household appliances. In contrast, outer rotor motors feature the rotor surrounding the stator, allowing for a larger surface area and improved cooling, often used in fans and larger industrial applications. Both types enhance efficiency and reduce energy consumption.</p></p>
