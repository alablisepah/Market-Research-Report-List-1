<p><p>The Diamond Abrasive Blades market comprises three primary types categorized by their manufacturing processes: Sintering, High-Frequency Welding, and Laser Welding. Sintering involves compacting diamond particles under high pressure and temperature, creating durable blades ideal for cutting hard materials. High-Frequency Welding joins diamond segments to a metal core using electromagnetic energy, providing robust performance for heavy-duty applications. Laser Welding utilizes focused laser beams for precise bonding, resulting in blades with high cutting efficiency and minimal thermal impact, suitable for intricate tasks.</p></p>
