<p><p>The multiparameter patient monitoring market comprises various devices essential for real-time health assessment. Bedside monitors continuously track vital signs like heart rate and oxygen levels, critical for inpatients. DCG monitors specifically assess cardiac functions and are vital during surgeries and emergency care. Intracranial pressure monitors gauge pressure within the skull, crucial for neurological patients. Cardiac defibrillator monitors provide real-time data during resuscitation efforts, ensuring effective treatment of arrhythmias or cardiac arrest, enhancing patient safety and outcomes.</p></p>
