<p><p>The TPU yarn market is categorized into two main types: One Component and Two Component. One Component TPU yarn consists of a single polymer formulation that simplifies production and application processes, making it user-friendly. In contrast, Two Component TPU yarn involves a combination of separate components that require mixing prior to use, offering enhanced customization and performance characteristics. Each type caters to different end-use applications, with One Component often suited for convenience, while Two Component allows for tailored solutions.</p></p>
