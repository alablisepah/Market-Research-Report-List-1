<p><p>Coarse Wavelength Division Multiplexing (CWDM) is a technology that enables the transmission of multiple optical signals over a single fiber optic cable by using distinct wavelengths. This method allows for efficient use of bandwidth, reducing costs for service providers and enhancing overall network capacity.
