<p><ul><li>Electric Vacuum Pumps</li><li>Mechanical Vacuum Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive vacuum pump market comprises two main types: electric vacuum pumps and mechanical vacuum pumps. Electric vacuum pumps utilize an electric motor to generate vacuum pressure, offering advantages such as improved efficiency, compact design, and quieter operation. They are increasingly adopted in modern vehicles due to their ability to support advanced functions like brake assistance and emissions control. Mechanical vacuum pumps, often driven by the engine, are traditional solutions that provide reliable vacuum but may be less efficient and noisier compared to their electric counterparts.</p></p>
