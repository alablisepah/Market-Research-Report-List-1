<p><p>The pharmaceutical excipients market includes various types that play crucial roles in drug formulation. Cellulose and its derivatives are used as binders and fillers, enhancing tablet integrity. Starch and its derivatives serve as disintegrants and viscosity regulators. Inorganic salts are employed for solubility and stability improvements. Medicinal sucrose functions as a sweetening agent and stabilizer, particularly in liquid formulations. Other excipients encompass a range of substances, including sugars, polymers, and surfactants, each contributing to drug effectiveness and delivery.</p></p>
