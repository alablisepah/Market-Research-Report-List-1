<p><p>Mango seed oil and butter are derived from the seeds of mangoes, widely used in cosmetics and food. The market is segmented into cold-pressed and expeller-pressed types. Cold-pressed extraction involves mechanically extracting oil at low temperatures, preserving nutrients and flavor, making it ideal for cosmetic applications. In contrast, expeller-pressed oil is extracted using higher temperatures and pressure, resulting in higher yield but potentially less nutritional value. Each method caters to different consumer preferences and applications in personal care and culinary uses.</p></p>
