<p><p>LiDAR (Light Detection and Ranging) is a crucial technology in the autonomous vehicle market, providing high-precision mapping and object detection. The market is primarily divided into two types: Solid State LiDAR and Mechanical/Scanning LiDAR. Solid State LiDAR utilizes a fixed design with no moving parts, offering enhanced durability and compact size, ideal for cost-sensitive applications. In contrast, Mechanical/Scanning LiDAR employs rotating components to capture 360-degree views, delivering high-resolution data but often at higher costs and complexity. Both types play a pivotal role in enabling safe navigation.</p></p>
