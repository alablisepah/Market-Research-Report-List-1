<p><p>The plasma etch system market for wafer processing includes several key technologies: Inductively Coupled Plasma (ICP), Reactive Ion Etching (RIE), and Deep Reactive Ion Etching (DRIE). ICP offers high plasma density for superior etching precision and control, making it ideal for intricate semiconductor structures. RIE combines chemical and physical processes for versatile etching across various materials. DRIE features a unique alternating process for achieving high aspect ratio features, essential in MEMS and advanced semiconductor applications, enabling detailed microfabrication work.</p></p>
