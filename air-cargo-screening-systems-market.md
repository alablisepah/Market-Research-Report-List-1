<p><strong>The Air Cargo Screening Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Explosive Detection Systems (EDS)</li><li>Explosive Trace Detection (ETD)</li><li>Non-computed Tomography Transmission X-ray (Non-CT X-ray)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Air Cargo Screening Systems market comprises several key technologies for ensuring cargo security. Explosive Detection Systems (EDS) utilize advanced imaging technology to identify potential explosives within cargo. Explosive Trace Detection (ETD) methods involve sampling materials for minute traces of explosives, enhancing threat detection. Non-computed Tomography Transmission X-ray (Non-CT X-ray) systems are simpler imaging technologies that provide quick scans of cargo, detecting anomalies without complex imaging, thus ensuring safer air transport while maintaining operational efficiency.</p></p>
