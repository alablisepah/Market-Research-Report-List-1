<p><strong>The Wire and Cable Recycling Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Manual Peeling</li><li>Mechanical Peeling</li><li>Mechanical Crushing</li><li>Chemical Method</li><li>Freezing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wire and cable recycling market comprises several methods for processing scrap materials. Manual peeling involves hand-stripping insulation from wires, while mechanical peeling employs machines to automate this process. Mechanical crushing reduces cables into smaller pieces for easier handling. The chemical method uses solvents to dissolve insulation, separating copper or aluminum from insulation material. Lastly, the freezing method involves cooling cables to brittle temperatures, allowing for easier separation of materials. Each method offers unique advantages depending on resource availability and sustainability goals.</p></p>
