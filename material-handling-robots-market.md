<p><p>The material handling robots market includes several types designed for specific tasks. Articulated material handling robots have multiple joints, enabling complex movements and versatility in various applications. SCARA (Selective Compliance Assembly Robot Arm) robots are ideal for horizontal movements and assembly tasks due to their stiff vertical arm, offering precision and speed. Parallel material handling robots feature a unique inverse-kinematics design, allowing rapid operation and high payload capacity, making them suitable for packaging and sorting applications.</p></p>
