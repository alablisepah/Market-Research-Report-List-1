<p><strong>The Car Wheel Speed Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hall Type</li><li>Magnetic Electric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car wheel speed sensor market consists primarily of two types: Hall effect sensors and magnetic electric sensors. Hall effect sensors utilize a semiconductor to detect changes in magnetic fields, providing precise speed readings, making them popular for advanced vehicle systems. In contrast, magnetic electric sensors operate on the principle of electromagnetic induction, generating voltage signals in response to wheel rotation. Both types are vital for vehicle stability control, anti-lock braking systems, and overall automotive safety, catering to diverse market needs.</p></p>
