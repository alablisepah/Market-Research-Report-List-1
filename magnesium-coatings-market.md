<p><p>The magnesium coatings market encompasses various types, each serving distinct applications. Cold coatings involve low-temperature application, enhancing surface properties without altering the substrate. Hot coatings utilize high temperatures for improved adhesion and durability. Thermoset coatings provide strong chemical and heat resistance, ideal for demanding environments. Electrolysis methods apply coatings through electrolytic processes, resulting in uniform finishes. Additionally, other methods include specialized coatings for unique requirements, expanding the versatility of magnesium coatings in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and electronics.</p></p>
