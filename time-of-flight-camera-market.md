<p><p>The Time-of-Flight (ToF) camera market primarily consists of two types: CMOS and CCD ToF cameras. CMOS ToF cameras leverage complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor technology, offering lower power consumption, faster frame rates, and compact designs, making them ideal for mobile devices and consumer electronics. Conversely, CCD ToF cameras utilize charge-coupled device technology, providing higher image quality and sensitivity, which is beneficial in applications like industrial automation and medical imaging. Each type caters to distinct needs based on performance and application requirements.</p></p>
