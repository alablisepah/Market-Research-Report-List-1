<p><p>Eccentric disk valves are specialized valves designed for efficient flow control in various applications. The market features two primary types: wafer type and lug type. Wafer type valves are compact and installed between two flanges, providing a lightweight solution ideal for space-constrained areas. Conversely, lug type valves have threaded lugs that allow for easy installation and disassembly, making them suitable for systems requiring regular maintenance. Both types serve specific needs in industries like water treatment, chemical processing, and HVAC systems.</p></p>
