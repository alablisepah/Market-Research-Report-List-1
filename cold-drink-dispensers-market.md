<p><p>Cold drink dispensers are categorized into two main types: mixer-type and spray-type. Mixer-type dispensers blend syrup and carbonated water, ensuring a consistent flavor in each serving. They are ideal for various beverages, including sodas and smoothies. On the other hand, spray-type dispensers deliver pre-mixed drinks by spraying a fine mist of beverage, which helps maintain carbonation and flavor. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and are widely utilized in restaurants, cafes, and convenience stores.</p></p>
