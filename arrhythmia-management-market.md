<p><p>The arrhythmia management market encompasses several key devices: pacemakers, defibrillators, and ablation devices. Pacemakers are implanted to regulate heart rhythms in patients with bradycardia, providing timely electrical stimulation. Defibrillators, including implantable and external types, deliver shocks to restore normal rhythm during life-threatening arrhythmias. Ablation devices utilize heat or cold to destroy abnormal heart tissue that causes irregular rhythms. Together, these technologies enhance the treatment landscape for arrhythmias, improving patient outcomes and quality of life.</p></p>
