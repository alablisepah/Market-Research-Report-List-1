<p><ul><li>Aspherical Microlens Array</li><li>Spherical Microlens Array</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fused silica microlens array market is segmented into aspherical and spherical microlens arrays. Aspherical microlens arrays feature non-uniform curvature, allowing for better light focusing, reducing spherical aberration, and enhancing image quality in applications like telecommunications and sensors. In contrast, spherical microlens arrays have uniform curvature, simplifying design and manufacturing while providing effective light collimation for applications such as fiber optics and illumination. Both types cater to diverse industries, including optics, electronics, and imaging technologies, addressing specific optical requirements.</p></p>
